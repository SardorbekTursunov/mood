Our Story

A Legacy of
Taste & Tradition

"Cooking is not just a craft — it is a language, and every plate tells a story."

Founded in 2014 by Chef Étienne Marchand, AURUM was born from a singular vision: to create an experience where time slows, flavours deepen, and every evening becomes a memory worth keeping. Nestled in the heart of the city, our dining room is a sanctuary of warmth and elegance.

We source exclusively from hand-selected farms and artisan producers, honouring seasonal rhythms and the integrity of each ingredient. Our team of twenty-two brings together culinary traditions from three continents, unified by an unwavering pursuit of excellence.
